💡 Priority Focus

✍️
Writing, Task 2 is key
Task 2 counts double. Aim for a clear position, 2 body paragraphs with supporting examples, and a strong conclusion. Minimum 250 words.
🎧
Listening, Section 4 focus
Academic monologues are the hardest section. Practise note-completion under strict conditions: one listen, no pausing.
📖
Reading, True/False/Not Given
The most common error source. "Not Given" means the text neither confirms nor denies the statement, don't guess from general knowledge.
🎤
Speaking, Extend your answers
Never give one-word answers. Use the PEEL method: Point → Example → Explanation → Link. Aim for 3-5 sentences per response.
