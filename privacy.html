Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

🦜 Short version: ParrotTalk does not create user accounts, does not sell your data, and stores your test scores only on your own device. Audio and writing submissions are processed temporarily by Google's AI and are not retained by ParrotTalk.

1. Who we are

ParrotTalk (parrottalk.app) is a free IELTS preparation platform. We provide mock tests, AI-powered writing and speaking feedback, and progress tracking for IELTS candidates worldwide.

2. Data we collect

Test scores and progress (stored locally on your device)

Your test scores, band results, and progress data are stored exclusively in your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers. You can clear this data at any time by clearing your browser's site data.

Audio recordings (Speaking module)

When you use the AI Speaking feedback feature, your voice is recorded locally in your browser. Your spoken answers are transcribed and the transcript is sent to an AI assistant for evaluation. No audio file is transmitted to or stored by ParrotTalk's servers. The AI service processes this data in accordance with its own privacy policy and terms of service.

Written text (Writing module)

When you request AI feedback on a writing task, your text is prepared as a prompt and sent to an AI assistant for evaluation. This data is not stored by ParrotTalk.

Email address (optional)

If you choose to subscribe to our newsletter, we collect your email address for the purpose of sending updates about new tests and features. You can unsubscribe at any time. We do not share your email with third parties.

3. What we do not collect

  • We do not create user accounts or store personal profiles
  • We do not use tracking cookies or advertising trackers
  • We do not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties
  • We do not store audio recordings or written essays on our servers
  • We do not use fingerprinting or behavioural tracking technologies

4. Third-party services

AI feedback service — Speaking transcripts and writing texts are sent to an external AI assistant for evaluation. ParrotTalk does not retain copies of data submitted for AI feedback.

Vercel — ParrotTalk is hosted on Vercel's infrastructure. Vercel may collect standard server logs (IP addresses, request times) in accordance with their own privacy policy. These logs are not used by ParrotTalk for tracking purposes.

5. Cookies

ParrotTalk does not use tracking cookies. We use browser localStorage to save your test scores and preferences locally. This is not a cookie and is not accessible to external services.

6. Your rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have the following rights:

  • Right of access — You can view all data stored locally by opening your browser's developer tools and inspecting localStorage under parrottalk.app
  • Right to erasure — You can delete all locally stored data at any time by clearing your browser's site data for parrottalk.app
  • Right to object — You can choose not to use the AI feedback features to avoid any data being sent to third-party APIs

Since ParrotTalk does not maintain a central database of user data, most GDPR rights are exercised directly through your browser rather than through a formal request to us.

7. Data retention

Audio recordings and written text submitted for AI feedback are not retained by ParrotTalk. Test scores and progress data stored in localStorage persist until you clear your browser data. Newsletter subscribers can request deletion of their email address at any time by unsubscribing or contacting us.

8. Children's privacy

ParrotTalk is intended for users aged 16 and over. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 16. IELTS is an examination typically taken by adults and older teenagers preparing for academic or professional purposes.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of ParrotTalk after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, you can reach us at parrottalk.app. We aim to respond to all privacy-related queries within 30 days.
