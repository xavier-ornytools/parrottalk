❓ FAQ

Common Questions

Is ParrotTalk really free?

Yes. No subscription, no credit card, no premium tier. Tests and core practice will always be free.

Do I need to create an account?

No sign-up required. Your progress is saved automatically in your browser. Just open the site and start practising.

What happens to my audio when I use Speaking AI?

Your voice is recorded locally in your browser. After recording, your spoken answers are transcribed and sent to an AI assistant for evaluation. No audio file is stored by ParrotTalk. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Is this for IELTS Academic or General Training?

All content is designed for IELTS Academic. The Reading passages, Writing tasks and Speaking questions match Academic exam standards. General Training candidates will also benefit from the practice, but the scoring targets the Academic band scale.

Does it work on mobile?

Yes. ParrotTalk is mobile-friendly. The Speaking module requires microphone access, which most modern mobile browsers support. For the best AI feedback experience, we recommend Chrome or Firefox.

We release new tests regularly. Subscribe to the newsletter below to be notified when new Listening, Reading, Writing and Speaking tests go live.
