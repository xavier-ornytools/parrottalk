📚 Grammar Revision — Recurring Speaking Errors

❌ Incorrect ✅ Correct Error Type
"make a master degree" "do a master's degree" make / do confusion
"make the turn of my life" "it was a turning point in my life" Collocation
"School need to..." "Schools need to..." Plural agreement
"I have started in 2014" "I started in 2014" Past simple (specific date)
"Since many years" "For many years" Since vs For
"I am agree" "I agree" Stative verb
