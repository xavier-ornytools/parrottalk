📚 Grammar Revision — Recurring Speaking Errors

❌ Incorrect ✅ Correct Error Type
"I am agree" "I agree" Stative verb
"Since 3 years" "For 3 years" Since vs For
"Informations are..." "Information is..." Uncountable noun
"I have went there" "I went there" Past participle
"More better" "Better" Double comparative
"The people is..." "People are..." Plural agreement
